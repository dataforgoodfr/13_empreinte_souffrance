The Suffering Footprint cannot be calculated

The product is not identified as fresh chicken eggs (in their shell)

In case of error, you can add in the product category the tags fresh eggs and the indicated breeding type(s) shown on the box: organic, free-range, barn, caged hens.

If the breeding type is not clearly displayed, you will find the possible breeding types in small letters in this format:

Producer code indicated on the egg:
0: organic – 1: free-range – 2: barn – 3: caged hens

You can also add the number of eggs in the product quantity and the caliber in the category.
